About Our School

Our Story

New Vision School was established with a vision to bring quality education to the heart of Aithole Village. We believe in holistic development, combining academic rigor with moral values.

Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that will enable them to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners.

Vision

To be a leading educational institution that inspires excellence and nurtures the unique potential of every child.
